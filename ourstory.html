Our Story

Pokoteke Canteen was born by three friends who had passion for cooking delicious meals for themselves and their colleagues at work.These friends were taxi drivers who had worked together for years.There were the founder members of A1 Taxis C0-operative Society in Eastleigh opposite V.I.D.It was started in the 1900 and is still serving good food up to today.Pokoteke canteen was recently renovated and renamed Pokoteke Jean's Kitchen in honour of our late mother. Pokoteke Jean's Kitchen offers good quality food and great dining experience.It is introducing breakfast, lunch and dinner meals for delievery,as well as great inhouse dining experience.The kitchen offers high quality food and our fantastic meals cater for every individual. Come and make it a family affair and enjoy our different meals. Pokoteke Jean's Kitchen focuses on giving our customers plenty of good food and healthy meals to choose between.It is a place for every occassion whether you want a quick breakfast with colleagues or you are celebrating a special birthday dinner with friends and family. Our value is to provide and serve good food to the community.
We hope you will love it as much as we do.
